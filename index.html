How The App Works?

Sign-Up

You sign up as an organisation, get a unique code on your provided email, register an administrator and register a classroom. You are now good to go and start using the iRegisterKids desktop apps.

Upload

Upload a picture when you have registered as an administrator. Each administrator can upload ther own picture too. You can either take your picture using the camera option or choose a saved picture on your device.

Create

You can create your account in just few seconds by creating and email and password as an organisation, saving your unique code which will be sent to your email, adding an administrator and registering a new class.

Device Activation

You can activate every device connected to your account as an administrator. Note that each administrator has each permissions which can limit users/administrators.
